Computed ingredients for dough:

  • ??g leaven
  • ??g AP flour (11.7% protein)
  • ??g WW flour (14% protein)
  • ??g water (92°F / 33°C)
  • ??g salt

Steps:

Mix and knead:

Mix water and flours. Autolyse for 15-45 min. Mix leaven. Knead until it begins showing gluten net. Add salt. Finish kneading.

Bulk Fermentation:

Make ball and let rest seam side down for 3+ hours.

Shaping:

Flour the dough and work surface, and flip the dough onto the work surface. Shape. Place dough ball into a semolina covered proofing basket, seam side up.

Proofing:

Cover with a towel and allow to rise to 2x size. When poked the dough ball should hold an indent briefly before bouncing back.

Baking:

  1. Preheat dutch oven in a 500°F / 260°C oven for 30-45min.
  2. Flip proofed dough ball onto parchment paper seam side down. Score, and spray water on top.
  3. Load parchment and dough into preheated dutch oven, reduce oven temperature to 485°F / 250°C and bake for 18 minutes.
  4. After 18 minutes, remove lid from dutch oven and sprinkle water again. Reduce oven temperature to 465°F / 240°C and bake for 25 more minutes.
