Steps:

  1. Place flour in a bowl, making a hole in the center. Break the whole eggs into that hole.
  2. Start mixing in eggs in the flour from the center outwards with a fork. When it gets too hard to use the fork, start kneading with your hands.
  3. Continue kneading until you have a smooth ball of dough. Then, wrap the dough ball in plastic wrap, and let it rest for 30min before shaping.
  4. To shape, cut the ball into 2N pieces. Work each piece into your desired pasta shape. While working each of the pieces, keep the rest of the dough covered in the wrap to prevent drying out. After working, cover the pasta in flour.
  5. Cook for a 3 to 5 minutes in boiling water, salted until it tastes like the sea.
