I am a Computer Science PhD Student at Carnegie Mellon University and Instituto Superior Técnico, where I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Inês Lynce and Professor Ruben Martins. I am interested in automated reasoning and program synthesis, particularly in their application to complex networked systems. In my PhD research, I am exploring the automatic synthesis of stateful programs based on noisy and incomplete execution traces.
